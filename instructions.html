Overview

The user study will consist of the following steps. The times below are estimates; however, you may take as much time as you like.

  • Background questionnaire (5 minutes)

  • Demonstration of the interface (4 minutes)

  • Practice using the interface [cars dataset] (5 minutes)

  • Refresher on basketball positions (3 minutes)

  • Study task [basketball dataset] (15-20 minutes)

  • Refresher on dog breeds (3 minutes)

  • Study task [dog breeds dataset] (15-20 minutes)

  • Post-study questionnaire (5 minutes)

Task

You will use the provided interactive visualization tool to explore a dataset of NBA basketball players and a dataset of individual dogs. Your task in this study is to use ALL of the data provided to assign each player to the position you believe they play and to assign each dog to the breed you think they belong to.

Ready?

Please complete the study in one session. Do not press 'Back' or 'Refresh' in the browser until you have completed the entire study.
